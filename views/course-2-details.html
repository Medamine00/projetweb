Graphic Design Student

Instructor: Lamis Touhami Duration: 4 Hours Price: 70DT

Course Description

This course is designed for aspiring graphic designers. You will learn the fundamentals of design, color theory, typography, and using software like Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Whether you are starting from scratch or looking to improve your skills, this course has something for you.

What You'll Learn

  • Basic Graphic Design Principles
  • Using Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • Color Theory and Typography
  • Creating Logos and Branding
  • Designing Marketing Materials (Brochures, Posters, etc.)
